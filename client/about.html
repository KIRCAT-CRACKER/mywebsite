About Me

A passionate cybersecurity student with a deep curiosity for ethical hacking, penetration testing, and protecting digital infrastructure in our increasingly connected world.

My Journey

My fascination with cybersecurity began during my early days of computer science studies when I encountered my first security vulnerability. The realization that a simple oversight in code could compromise an entire system sparked my passion for understanding and preventing cyber threats.

Currently pursuing my degree at Softwarica College in partnership with Coventry University, I've dedicated myself to mastering the art of ethical hacking and cybersecurity. Every day brings new challenges and learning opportunities that fuel my determination to become a skilled cybersecurity professional.

Beyond academics, I spend countless hours on platforms like TryHackMe and Hack The Box, honing my penetration testing skills and staying updated with the latest security trends. My goal is to contribute meaningfully to the cybersecurity community and help organizations protect their digital assets.
